Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
You can access lessons, exercises, quizzes, and other learning resources without paying.
3. Do I need to create an account?
You can start learning without an account, but creating a free account is recommended.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
Build your English vocabulary by learning a few new words daily, practicing them in sentences, and reviewing regularly.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Yes — you can offer free digital certificates that auto-generate after learners complete a course.
